About

Fuel the Fire was founded by Katya Black, a marketing executive with 20 years of experience leading growth strategy, brand, and go-to-market for some of the world's most recognized companies — including YouTube, Google, Sephora, and Airbnb.

Katya also co-founded MedBio Institute, a B2B health platform built to help independent clinics in the metabolic health space grow through strategic education, revenue optimization, and industry partnerships — giving Fuel the Fire direct operating experience inside the provider ecosystem it serves.

Fuel the Fire is built specifically for the GLP-1, peptide, longevity, and metabolic health space. That focus is deliberate. These markets move fast, operate under complex regulatory constraints, and require a level of ecosystem access that generalist consultancies don't have. We do.

Katya is also the founder of Wellness Growth Lab, where she shares practical insights on marketing, AI, and business strategy for the modern wellness industry.
